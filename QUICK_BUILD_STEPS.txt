iPad Build Quick Steps:
=======================

1. In Xcode Navigator (left sidebar):
   ✓ Click the blue "App" icon (you can see this!)

2. In the center panel tabs:
   ✓ Click "Signing & Capabilities"
   ✓ Select your Team

3. In top bar:
   ✓ Choose "iPad Pro" simulator
   ✓ Press ⌘R (Command + R)

That's it! The app should build and launch on iPad simulator.
